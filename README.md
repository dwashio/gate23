# GATE 23 — airport lounge ambience

Work, read, and focus from a window seat at a pixel-art airport.

**▶ Live: https://dwashio.github.io/gate23/**

ドット絵の空港ラウンジで作業するためのambienceサイトです。窓の外では飛行機が離着陸し、空は実時刻と連動して朝焼けから星空へ変わり、ときどき雨が降ります。

## Features

- **Soundscape** — real airport crowd murmur, lounge jazz through "venue speakers", and PA announcements with chimes (crowd ducks under the PA, like the real thing)
- **Your Flight** — a boarding-pass pomodoro. Check in for 25/50/90 minutes; your flight KL867 to Amsterdam appears on the departures board, boards, makes its final call, and when your session ends **your plane actually pushes back and takes off**
- **Passport** — flight stamps for every completed session, plus a sightings dex of 12 rare encounters (a late runner, an escaped cat, a rainbow jet, a UFO...)
- **Living world** — time-of-day sky synced to your clock, weather, baggage tugs, jet bridges, travellers with rolling suitcases

Everything is a single HTML file. No build, no tracking, no account.
